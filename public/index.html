Do Won Kim

I am a 1st year Ph.D. student in Information Studies at University of Maryland. I am advised by Giovanni Luca Ciampaglia and Cody Buntain.

My research interest is in the intersection of computational social science and political science. I study relationship between digital media and political behavior, with a particular focus on how the information environment and algorithms shape political beliefs, identities, and behaviors. I employ a wide range of quantitative methods in my research, including causal inference and machine learning.
